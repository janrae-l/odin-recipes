Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti and meatball recipe is the ultimate weeknight family dinner â€” quick, easy, and picky eater-approved!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti pasta (16 ounces)
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 pound ground beef (or ground pork)
  • 4 pieces red hot dog (sliced diagonally)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 bulb of red onion
  • 1 can El Rancho Meatball sauce
  • 1 kilo Sweet Style Spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Boil enough water using a large saucepan and cook the pasta according to the package instructions. You can usually see it at the back of the bag. Save a cup of the pasta water and drain the excess using a colander.
  2. In a skillet with 2 taablespoons of cooking oil, saute the garlic, onion, and bell pepper until translucent and aromatic. Then add the ground beef, salt, and black pepper, and stir everything together with a spatula. Be sure to break the ground beef into bite-size pieces. Let the meat cook until tender and lightly brown.
  3. Pour the 1 kilo of Sweet Style spaghetti sauce, pasta water, and 1 can of El Rancho Meatball sauce and mix everything. Let it bring to a gentle boil and lower the heat to simmer for about 30-45 minutes or until the sauce is thickened. Be sure to cover with a lid.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti noodles; stir everything to combine. Add more seasoning according to your taste. Turn off the heat. Serve and enjoy!
